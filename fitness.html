Why Fitness is Important for Women

  • Boosts Mental Health: Exercise helps reduce anxiety and improve mood.
  • Improves Strength: Builds bone density and core stability, especially important as women age.
  • Manages Weight: Regular activity helps maintain a healthy body composition.
  • Regulates Hormones: Physical activity supports menstrual and hormonal balance.
  • Empowers Confidence: A strong body leads to a strong mind and higher self-esteem.

Fitness Tips Every Woman Should Follow

  • Mix cardio & strength: Combine walking, cycling, or dancing with resistance workouts.
  • Stay consistent: It's better to exercise regularly than to go all-out once a week.
  • Warm-up & stretch: Always prepare your body and avoid injuries.
  • Hydrate well: Drink water before, during, and after workouts.
  • Listen to your body: Rest when needed and don't overtrain.

  • 7 Minute Workout: Great for beginners short on time.
